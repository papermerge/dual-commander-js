Dual Commander

This feature demo focuses on correct behaviour of dual commander within context of several documents and folders. You should be able navigate each commander panel by clicking respective folder (in panel and in breadcrumb). When clicking a document - it must open in other panel. In the opened viewer you should be able to zoom in/zoom out pages. Also, by clicking a folder in viewer's breadcrumb the viewer will 'close' and commander will replace it.
